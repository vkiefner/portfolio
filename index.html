Research Agenda

How do multinational enterprises navigate strategy and sustainability under conditions of geopolitical tension, geo-economic fragmentation, societal contestation, and persistent global development challenges?

My research combines international business strategy, supply chain governance, and organizational leadership to understand:

  • MNE Sustainability Behavior: When and why firms support the UN SDGs; how leadership diversity influences sustainability-oriented decision-making
  • Global Value Chain Resilience: How supply base diversification and complexity affect firm performance under disruption
  • Strategy Under Uncertainty: How firms respond to nationalist and polarized environments; how digital technologies reshape global coordination
